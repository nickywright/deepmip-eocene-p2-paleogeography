# Readme for Montheil++_2026_ESR

This folder is related to Montheil et al. (2026).
The paleogeography used is not included here due to its size, but can be downloaded from their supplement (https://doi.org/10.6084/m9.figshare.29558477.v1)).
This workflow specifically uses their Scenario 2 (https://ndownloader.figshare.com/files/56241896)

The rotation file can be obtained in their supplement as well (https://ndownloader.figshare.com/files/56243390 for direct access).

Included here are the static polygons used for rotating parts of the grid, as the plate IDs are very similar and the polygons in their supplement did not cover all regions required.
