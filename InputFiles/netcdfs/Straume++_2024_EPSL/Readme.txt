# Readme for Straume++_2024_EPSL

This is a copy of Straume et al.' (2024) paleogeography for 51 and 56 Ma. These netcdfs were obtained from the Zenodo associated with their paper (https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.8262689)

Full reference: Straume, E.O., Steinberger, B., Becker, T.W. and Faccenna, C., 2024. Impact of mantle convection and dynamic topography on the Cenozoic paleogeography of Central Eurasia and the West Siberian Seaway. Earth and Planetary Science Letters, 630, p.118615. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.epsl.2024.118615
