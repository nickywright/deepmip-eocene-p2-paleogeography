# Readme for He++_2019_ScienceChinaEarthSciences

This folder contains the two (High Tibetan Plateau and Low Tibetan Plateau) Early Eocene paleogeographies from He et al. (2019)

Reference: 
He, Z., Zhang, Z. and Guo, Z., 2019. Reconstructing early Eocene (∼ 55 Ma) paleogeographic boundary conditions for use in paleoclimate modelling. Science China Earth Sciences, 62(9), pp.1416-1427. https://doi.org/10.1007/s11430-019-9366-2
